Frequently

Questions

What is Tharwah Academy?

Tharwah Academy is the training and development arm of Tharwah, offering specialized learning experiences designed to empower individuals and organizations.

What services does Tharwah Academy offer?

We offer a variety of training tracks and HR certifications, in addition to a comprehensive range of customized learning solutions for organizations, covering areas such as coaching, training consulting services, and professional skills development.

Who delivers the training courses at Tharwah Academy?

Training is delivered by experienced professionals and certified experts with deep knowledge in their respective fields.

Do you work with individuals or only organizations?

We support both individuals looking to grow professionally and organizations seeking to develop their workforce capabilities.

Can training courses be customized based on the organizations needs?

Yes, we offer customized training courses tailored to your companys nature and employee levels to ensure maximum benefit.

Do you offer in-person or virtual training?

We offer flexible training formats, including in-person, virtual, and blended learning to suit different needs and preferences.

Does the Academy provide accredited certificates upon completion of training courses?

Yes, we collaborate with internationally recognized training institutions to ensure that participants receive globally accredited certificates.

Where are your offices located?

Our head office is in Riyadh, in the Al-Narjis district, and we proudly serve our clients from additional locations in Jeddah, Dammam, Dubai, and Egypt.
